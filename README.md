# OPTIMIZE – Digital Performance Agency

A landing page for **OPTIMIZE**, a digital company specialized in image optimization, website creation, and high-performance landing pages.

## 🔗 Live Site

[optimize-project.vercel.app](https://optimize-project.vercel.app)

## 📄 About

OPTIMIZE helps businesses improve their digital presence by delivering faster websites, lighter images, and landing pages built to convert. The site presents the company's services, pricing plans, and a contact form integrated with WhatsApp.

## 📌 Sections

- **Home** – Hero section with a before/after image comparison slider
- **About** – Company overview and value proposition
- **Plans** – Four service tiers (Bronze, Silver, Gold, Diamond) covering image optimization, web development, and support
- **App** – Call-to-action for downloading the mobile app (App Store & Play Store)
- **Contact** – Modal form with WhatsApp integration
